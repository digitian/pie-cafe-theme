We have a clean, lid dining room

Pie Cafe's cozy dining room offers a warm, inviting atmosphere perfect for any meal. Enjoy friendly service, fresh flavors, and a comfortable space to relax and dine.

We have a very large pizza menu for your lunch, dinner, and catering needs.

We craft our pizzas from scratch. The dough made daily in the house. We are also the home of Oven Baked Subs and Wings-On-The-Spectrum. We serve crispy, juicy jumbo chicken wings with 12 different flavors. unbeatable prices, too! We hand bread the chicken tenders, bone-in and boneless wings, pressure deep fried for crispy outside & juicy inside deliciousness.
